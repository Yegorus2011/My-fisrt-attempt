As you may have been able to infer, this is a website dedicated to Linguomania, an app for language learning. It allows you to improve your level of proficiency in a specific language over the course of a specified time. Using this application, you shall be able to expand the horizons of your understanding of not only the language itself, but also of a different culture from hundreds of miles away. We sincerely hope that you share our excitement and that you are prepared to venture into the unknown right alongside us.
